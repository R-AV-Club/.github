# R&AV Club

### Robotics • Automation • Autonomous Systems • Innovation

R&AV Club is a multidisciplinary technical club focused on designing, developing, and documenting innovative engineering projects across robotics, embedded systems, autonomous technologies, and competitive engineering platforms.

The organization serves as a collaborative ecosystem where students learn practical engineering skills through hands-on development, technical research, competitions, workshops, and real-world problem solving beyond academics.

# Competitions & Achievements

R&AV Club has actively participated in:

- International level competitions
- National level competitions
- State level technical events
- Engineering exhibitions

Over the years, the club and its members have secured multiple awards, recognitions, and significant prize winnings through competitive engineering and technical excellence.

# Learning & Development

Apart from project development, the club regularly conducts:

- Technical workshops
- Internal competitions
- Skill development sessions
- Knowledge sharing activities
- Hands-on training programs
- Team collaboration events

These activities help members strengthen practical skills, encourage innovation, and maintain a healthy competitive learning environment.

---

# Open Engineering Culture

R&AV Club believes in learning through experimentation, documentation, collaboration, and continuous improvement. By maintaining open and structured project repositories, the organization aims to build a long-term engineering knowledge ecosystem for current and future members.
